🚀 MNRV Quick Start - Deploy in 15 Minutes

The Fastest Way to Get Online

Step 1: Choose Hosting (2 minutes)

Recommended: Netlify (easiest, free, fast)

  1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
  2. That’s it! Keep browser open.

Step 2: Deploy Your Site (3 minutes)

  1. Drag & Drop Method:
    • Open Windows Explorer
    • Navigate to: C:\Users\info\OneDrive\Bureaublad\MNRV
    • Select ALL files and folders
    • Drag them to the Netlify drop zone
    • Wait 30 seconds
    • Your site is LIVE! 🎉
  2. Note your URL:
    • Netlify gives you: https://random-name.netlify.app
    • Bookmark this!

Step 3: Connect Your Domain mnrv.nl (5 minutes)

  1. In Netlify:
    • Click “Domain settings”
    • Click “Add custom domain”
    • Enter: mnrv.nl
    • Click “Verify”

  2. Netlify shows you DNS records. Copy them.

  3. Go to your domain registrar (where you bought mnrv.nl):
    • Login
    • Find DNS settings
    • Add the records Netlify gave you
    • Save
  4. Wait 10-60 minutes for DNS to update
  5. Visit https://mnrv.nl - IT WORKS! ✨

Step 4: Submit to Google (5 minutes)

  1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
  2. Add property: https://mnrv.nl
  3. Verify ownership (follow Google’s steps)
  4. Submit sitemap: https://mnrv.nl/sitemap.xml
  5. Click “Request Indexing” for main pages

DONE! 🎊

Emergency Checklist

If something doesn’t work:

  • Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
  • Check DNS with dnschecker.org
  • Wait longer (DNS can take 24-48 hours)
  • Try incognito/private browsing mode
  • Check Netlify deploy logs
  • Verify files uploaded correctly
